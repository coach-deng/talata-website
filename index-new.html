Programs

Find your path

Three tracks. One standard. Whether you want to compete at the highest level, join a team, or build your game from scratch.

Academy

5 sessions per week. Video analysis. Strength and conditioning. Scholarship pathway. Led by Coach Deng. For players who want to go somewhere with this.

5x / Week Video Analysis Scholarship Path

Teams

No experience needed. 2 to 3 sessions per week. League games every weekend. Ages 6 to 19. Just come ready to learn and compete.

2-3x / Week League Games Ages 6-19

Development

1-on-1 with a coach. Flexible scheduling. Your own personalized program. All ages, all levels. Built around what you need.

1-on-1 Flexible All Ages
