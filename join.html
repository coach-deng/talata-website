Questions

Common questions.

Does my child need experience to join?

No. The Teams program starts from zero. No experience, no tryouts. Just register and show up on Thursday. The coaches have time for beginners.

How much does membership cost?

Fees are set at the General Assembly and vary by program. Deng will walk you through the options after your free trial. No surprises.

What age groups do you have?

We have programs from age 6 (U9) all the way through senior. Academy is U15 and U17. Teams run U9 through U19. Everyone 20 and up plays for Talata Men.

What is the free trial?

Free trials every Thursday at Svanemøllehallen — U15 and under at 16:15, U17 and older at 18:00. From May, we also run Saturday sessions 11:00–13:00. One full session, completely free. Register so we know you are coming.

My child is serious about basketball. Is this the right club?

Yes. Talata has already placed a player at Orangeville Prep in Canada. The Academy program runs 5 sessions a week with film sessions, shooting machines, and direct scholarship pathway support. Deng played pro in Canada, Lithuania, and Denmark. He knows the path.

Can I come watch a session before registering?

Absolutely. Come to Svanemøllehallen any Thursday (U15 & under at 16:15, U17+ at 18:00) or Saturday from May at 11:00. No need to call ahead. For Academy tryouts, register below first so we can set aside time.
