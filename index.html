What we offer

Pick your path.

Three programs, one club. Pick what fits your level and how serious you want to get.

Most popular
▲

Academy

For players who are serious about the game. Five sessions a week, shooting machines, sprint work, and mental coaching. If you want to play at the next level, this is where you start.

  • 5 sessions per week
  • Video analysis & feedback
  • Scholarship pathway support
  • Led by Coach Deng Awak
Apply for Academy
●

Teams

Two to three practices a week. League games, real teammates, and coaches who actually show up. You don't have to be elite — you just have to want to play.

  • 2–3 sessions per week
  • League games included
  • All levels welcome
  • Ages 10–25
Join a Team
■

Development

One coach, one player. We find what's holding you back and fix it — shooting form, footwork, defense, decision-making. Fits around your schedule.

  • Flexible scheduling
  • Personalized program
  • Shooting, defense, footwork
  • All ages
