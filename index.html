What we offer

Pick your path.

Three programs, one club. Pick what fits your level and how serious you want to get.

Most popular

Academy

For players who are serious about the game. Five sessions a week, shooting machines, sprint work, and mental coaching. If you want to play at the next level, this is where you start.

  • 5 sessions per week
  • Video analysis & feedback
  • Scholarship pathway support
  • Led by Coach Deng Awak
Apply for Academy

Teams

Never touched a basketball? That's fine. We start from zero. Two to three sessions a week, real games, real teammates and coaches who actually have time for you. No tryouts. No experience needed. Just show up.

  • No experience needed
  • 2 to 3 sessions per week
  • League games included
  • Ages 6 to 19
Join a Team

Development

One coach, one player. We find what's holding you back and fix it. Shooting form, footwork, defense, decision-making. Fits around your schedule.

  • Flexible scheduling
  • Personalized program
  • Shooting, defense, footwork
  • All ages

The staff

Your coaches.

Everyone on this staff has played the game at a high level. That's the standard.

Head Coach

Deng Awak

Deng played pro ball in Canada, Lithuania, and Denmark, including the Danish BasketLigaen with BK Amager. He captained his team at Mount Royal University in Calgary. Now he's in Copenhagen building something real. Player development, scholarship placements, and the international network to back it up.

coach@talatabasketball.dk
GJ
Head of Youth Program

Georg Jespersen

Film study, defensive systems, game prep. If there's tape from your last game, Georg has already broken it down twice.

TZ
Youth Coach (U9 to U13)

Tobias Zahle

Works with the youngest players from U9 to U13. Focused on development and the details that don't show up in the box score. Standards on and off the court.

JY
Strength & Conditioning

James Young

Played pro in the Danish BasketLigaen with Basketball Club Copenhagen and BK Amager, where he and Deng were teammates in 2021. Now head trainer at Sporting Health Club in Copenhagen. He's been through the grind. He knows how to build players who hold up all season long.

JN
International Scout & Online Coach

Jordan Naughton

6'10" former Division I center at the University of Wyoming. Played pro in Denmark with BK Amager. Now he scouts internationally and runs our online development program. He is the pipeline from Copenhagen to North America.

JH
International Academy Coach & Scout

Jacob Hoffman

Danish-born, raised in Horsens. Jacob played college ball in the USA at Bemidji State, shooting 44% from three. Then went pro back in Denmark with Randers Cimbria, BC Copenhagen, and Svendborg Rabbits. Now based in Florida coaching at an international basketball academy. He keeps Talata connected to the North American scene and scouts talent on both sides of the Atlantic.

LA
School Programs Coach

Lens Aboudou

Lens is currently away playing pro ball and will be back. He coaches our school programs when he is in Copenhagen. The kind of coach kids actually listen to because he is out there doing it himself.
