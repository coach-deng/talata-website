What is Talata Academy?

Most kids play basketball once or twice a week. Academy kids play almost every day. Monday through Friday mornings and afternoons, games on the weekend. We build real players here.

It's not just basketball. We do strength and conditioning with James. Film sessions so players understand the game, not just run through it. Travel to other countries to play teams they would never see in Denmark. That's how development actually happens.

The whole thing is built around one idea. Basketball is the tool we use to prepare kids for whatever life throws at them next. Discipline, teamwork, showing up when it's hard, handling losses, handling wins. That's the real curriculum.

Six team sessions a week, all at Svanemøllehallen. Mornings before school, afternoons after, and Saturday mornings. Games on the weekends.
