What is Talata Mini?

We started Talata Mini because a lot of parents were asking about their younger kids. The older programs were filling up and parents wanted something for the 6, 7, 8, 9-year-olds who just want to play.

So here it is. Twice a week at Strandvejsskolen. Small group, real coaching, age-appropriate games and activities. The whole point is to get kids moving, get them comfortable with a ball, and make sure they actually enjoy it.

At this age, the most important thing is that kids want to come back next week. That's what we're building toward.

🏃

Movement first

Kids this age need to move. Every session is built around activity — running, jumping, games — not just standing in lines.

🤝

Teamwork

They learn to play with others, take turns, cheer each other on. Small things that matter a lot at this age.

🏀

Ball control

Dribbling, passing, shooting — all broken down to what a 7-year-old can actually do and build on over time.

😄

Just have fun

We are not trying to make U9 players into pros. We want them to love the game. The rest comes later.
