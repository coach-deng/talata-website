What is Talata Mini?

For the 6 to 10-year-olds who just want to play. Come once or twice a week, whichever works for your family. Real coaching, small group, age-appropriate games.

The only thing that matters at this age is that your kid wants to come back next week. That's what we build toward.

Movement first

Every session is built around activity. Running, jumping, games. Not standing in lines.

Teamwork

Taking turns, cheering each other on. Small things that matter a lot at this age.

Ball control

Dribbling, passing, shooting. Broken down to what a 7-year-old can actually do.

Just have fun

We want your kid to love the game. Everything else comes later.

First time? Perfect.

Most kids who join have never played before. That's exactly who this is for.

We know your kid's name

Small group. Coaches know every kid by name, how they learn, what they need.

A good place to start: Spring Camp

If your kid has never touched a basketball before, the Spring Camp is one of the best ways in. Two days, no pressure, surrounded by other kids at the same level.

A lot of kids who come to camp end up joining the regular Mini sessions after. By that point the coaches already know them, which makes the transition really easy.

What's next at 11?

When your kid turns 11, they graduate from Mini into Talata U13 — Talata's first competitive team tier. Same club, same coaches, bigger game.

U13 trains with a proper schedule, plays league games, and is where the basketball really starts to develop. Kids who go through Mini arrive already knowing the system, which gives them a real head start.

Mini → U13: The path is automatic. When your child is ready, we'll reach out and walk you through the move to Holdsport and the U13 team.
