About the Camp

Nida sits on the Curonian Spit, a UNESCO-listed strip of land between the Baltic Sea and the Curonian Lagoon. This is a partner camp in Nida. Serious basketball in one of the most unique locations in Europe. Your player trains hard, then walks out to the Baltic coast. There is nothing else like it.

Partner Camp in Nida

All meals, accommodation, and coaching included. A full week of basketball with the Baltic Sea right there.

What's Included

Flights

Return flights from Copenhagen (CPH) to Kaunas (KUN) are included in the price. We book as a group.

Accommodation

Stay at the camp site in Nida. Shared rooms. All meals covered: breakfast, lunch, dinner.

Training

3 sessions a day. Position work, 5v5, and evening shooting lab. Coaches who take it seriously.

Camp Kit

Every player gets the Nida Camp kit: t-shirt and shorts. Bring extra training gear for the week. Additional merch available on site.

Health Insurance

Travel health insurance included. Bring your blue EU Health Card as backup.
