About this camp

Two days in the gym built for younger players. Dribbling, shooting, footwork, and team games every session. Everything is age-appropriate: small court, size 5 ball, and coaches who know how to work with this age group.

All levels welcome. Whether your kid just picked up a ball or has been playing for a couple of years, they will fit in and be challenged.
