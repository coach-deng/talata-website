About the Camp

Basketball is basically a national sport in Lithuania. We're sending our players to train with BasketNews Camp, the biggest youth camp in the Baltics, 9 years running. Your kid trains alongside Lithuanian, Latvian, and international players. Real competition, real development.

🏀

BasketNews Camp

Top-level facilities. 200+ international players every year. Indoor gym in Karpyne, outdoor sessions on the Baltic coast in Nida. The coaching here is serious.

What's Included

Training

2-3 sessions a day. Work on your position. Scrimmage against international players. Get real feedback from coaches who've been doing this for years.

Accommodation

Stay at the camp site in Karpyne. Shared rooms. Every meal is covered: breakfast, lunch, dinner.

Travel

We book the flights (CPH → Vilnius). All transport in Lithuania is sorted. Airport pickup included.

Experience

Day trip to Nida on the Baltic coast. Team bonding, beach time, and your kid comes home with a BasketNews Camp certificate.

Full Talata Kit

Every player gets the full Talata Lithuania Camp kit: training top, shorts, shirt, and socks. Additional merch available to purchase.
