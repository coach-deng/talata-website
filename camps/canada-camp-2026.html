About the Trip

We're taking 12 players to Canada to train at a real university gym, go up against Canadian teams, and get coached by college-level staff. It's 9 days of full-time basketball in a country that takes the game seriously.

We're partnering with Coach Kenny Otieno (University of Lethbridge Pronghorns) and Coach Ryan Heggie (Lethbridge College) — they'll be running sessions alongside our staff.
